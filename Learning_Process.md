# Learning Process

## Question 1
### What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.
The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you understand a concept by explaining it in simple words as if you are teaching someone else.

## Question 3
### What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
Active (focused) mode is when we concentrate deeply on a task and try to solve problems step by step. It is used when we are studying or working on something important.
Diffuse mode is when our mind is relaxed and not focusing directly on the problem. In this mode, the brain makes connections in the background and helps us understand things better.
