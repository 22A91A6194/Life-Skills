## Question 2
### How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P?
B = MAP means Behavior happens when Motivation, Ability, and Prompt come together at the same time.

- M (Motivation) – The desire or willingness to do something
- A (Ability) – How easy the habit is to do
- P (Prompt) – A reminder or trigger that tells us to do the habit

We can make new habits easier by starting with simple actions, staying motivated, and using reminders consistently.

### Why is it important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of a habit?
Celebrating after completing a habit helps our brain connect the activity with positive feelings. When we feel happy or proud after doing something, our brain remembers it and makes us want to repeat the habit again.
