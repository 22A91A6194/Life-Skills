# Focus Management

### Question 1:
### What is Deep Work?

- Deep Work is a focused state where you do important tasks without distractions  
- It helps you learn difficult things faster  
- It improves the quality of your output  
- It requires full concentration for a fixed period of time  
- It is useful for learning

---

### Question 2:
### According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

- Work in a distraction-free environment  
- Focus on one task at a time  
- Schedule fixed time blocks for deep work  
- Avoid multitasking  
- Take regular breaks to maintain energy  
- Train the mind to focus like a skill  
- Remove phone and social media distractions  

---

### Question 3:
### How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- Set daily fixed study/work hours  
- Turn off notifications during focus time  
- Use techniques like Pomodoro or timed sessions  
- Keep a clean and distraction-free workspace  
- Break big tasks into smaller steps  
- Start with small focus sessions and increase gradually  
- Track progress daily  

---

## Dangers of Social Media

### Question 4:
### What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- Reduces attention span  
- Causes addiction and time waste  
- Increases comparison and low self-esteem  
- Disturbs sleep patterns  
- Spreads misinformation easily  
- Creates distraction from real-life goals  
- Increases anxiety and stress
