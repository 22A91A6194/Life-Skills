## 4. Sleep is your superpower

### Question 4:
Paraphrase the “Sleep is your Superpower” video in brief. Only points.

- Sleep improves memory and learning
- Proper sleep keeps the brain healthy
- Lack of sleep affects concentration and mood
- Sleep strengthens the immune system
- Good sleep improves emotional balance
- Deep sleep helps body recovery and repair
- Poor sleep increases risk of diseases
- Sleep improves creativity and decision-making
- Consistent sleep schedule is important
- Adults generally need around 7–8 hours of sleep

---

### Question 5:
What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?

- Sleep and wake up at the same time daily
- Avoid mobile phones before sleep
- Reduce caffeine at night
- Keep the room dark and quiet
- Exercise regularly
- Avoid heavy meals before bedtime
- Practice meditation or deep breathing
- Reduce overthinking before sleep
- Limit screen time at night
- Get sunlight exposure during the day

## 5. Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise

### Question 6:

- Exercise improves brain function
- Physical activity increases memory power

### Question 7:
What are some steps you can take to exercise more?

- Start with small daily walks
- Use stairs instead of lifts
- Follow a simple workout routine
- Play outdoor sports regularly
- Stretch or move during study/work breaks
- Exercise with friends for motivation
- Set small fitness goals
- Do yoga or pranayama daily
- Reduce long sitting hours
- Make exercise part of daily routine
